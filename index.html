About NeoFolks

Our Growing Tech Community

NeoFolks is a vibrant and rapidly growing tech community at Navrachana University, where passionate students, developers, and tech enthusiasts come together to learn, innovate, and build the future of technology. We foster an environment of collaboration, creativity, and continuous learning.

From organizing hands-on workshops and hackathons to facilitating open-source contributions and peer learning sessions, we're committed to empowering every member with the skills and connections needed to excel in the tech industry.
